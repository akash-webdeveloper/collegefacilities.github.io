OUR FACILITIES

Atal Bihari Vajpayee Government Institute of Engineering & Technology was established in 10 april 2011. It is the Second Government Regional Engineering institute of Himachal Pradesh which is located at Pragatinagar Village Gumma, Teh. Kotkhai, Distt. Shimla on the bank of river Giri Ganga about 60 km from Shimla on Shimla Rohru National Highway.
